Biography

On June 28, 2003, a legend was born. Born in Des Moines, Iowa (bro what goes on out there), Miss Amanda Grace Hurst quite honestly didn't live there long, as she spent her K-12 years in Fairfax County. Amanda loves gardening, baking, and listening to garbage pop, like the kind of pop music that you hear in an Urban Outfitters on a Thursday afternoon. Anyways, my babygirl Amanda also has a Beagle named Daisy, and a 13-year-old brother that she loves very much.

Quick facts: Amanda's favorite season is spring, her favorite artist is Harry Styles, she has this weird crush on Jon Ossoff, her favorite holiday is Halloween, she would move to Paris or Rome if she could, and she doesn't like peas or lima beans.
